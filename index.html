About Us

Handcrafted beverages, made fresh every single day

Since 1998, Shree Ram Colddrinks has been serving the freshest, most flavorful cold beverages in town. Every drink is crafted from scratch using fresh ingredients, our signature pure sugar syrups, and absolutely no preservatives. What started as a small shop has grown into a beloved local destination — trusted by thousands of customers who keep coming back for that authentic, pure sugar taste.

1984

Every single lemon was hand-pressed and served with a smile. This became the foundation of our commitment to freshness.

OUR ORIGINS

The Soda Cart Origins

In the spring of 1984, our founder started a humble hand-squeezed soda cart on the streets of Surat, serving fresh, chilled soda with local citrus fruits and secret spice blends.

1998

Transitioning from a cart to a registered local brand while preserving the original family recipe.

FIRST PERMANENT SHOP

First Storefront & Bottling

In 1998, we established our first permanent brick-and-mortar shop. This landmark year marked the birth of bottling our signature pure sugar syrups to share with the neighborhood.

2005

Expanding the family recipe card to cater to changing taste trends without losing our heritage.

MENU EXPANSION

50+ Flavors Introduced

We diversified our offering to include mojitos, premium lassis, and thick milkshakes, bridging modern taste preferences with traditional, time-tested recipes.

2015

Improving comfort and layout standards, serving thousands of guests daily with unmatched consistency.

PREMIUM STANDARDS

Modern Outlets & Hygiene

We upgraded our locations to premium, hygienic modern outlets. We integrated advanced cooling equipment to keep every glass pure, refreshing, and entirely preservative-free.

today

40+ years of legacy, 100+ flavors, and a family of loyal patrons — still handcrafted, still fresh.

LA VITA. LA PASSIONE.

A Legacy of Handcrafted Love

The experiences of our long past have in no way slowed down our growth. Rather, they serve as a springboard for an innovative, passionate spirit that every member of the Shree Ram team embodies today.
